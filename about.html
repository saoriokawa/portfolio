MY STORY

Before I stepped into the world of UX, I was always into learning new languages which allowed me to communicate with various people and be open-minded. Eventually, I decided to become a language teacher, like many other language-lovers do.

As a teacher, first I listened to and observed students to figure out what they struggle to understand, then organised the relevant information in a simple way to help them comprehend it more easily. Without realising that this skill could be useful as a UX Designer, I was following my linguistic interests for many years. I was not unhappy, but I had a feeling that something was missing.

It was when I was making a website for my self-employment work that I finally met the field of UX. The more I studied, the more I was absorbed in this field. I was fascinated with the fact that a small change of design could improve so many people’s lives.

Since 2019, I have been working as a UX Designer. Now I am looking to give positive impacts on the various users’ lives through design.
